About Me...

Welcome to the About Me page, my name is Nathan Moudakis, but most of my close friends will just call me Nate. I was born in California and while I have always lived in California, my life adventures have had me living from ocean to valley to mountains, which is where I am now living in South Lake Tahoe.

Education...

I graduated from California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo in June of 2021, with a dual concentration in accounting and computer information systems. My accounting coursework at Cal Poly enabled me to develop a deep understanding of the business environment and ability to read & understand the financial health of a company or industry based on financial information and forecast that data on both a micro and macro scale. Through my studies of information systems I developed a highly sophisticated understanding of networks, centralization and decentralization as well as the history of the internet from ARPANET to Web 3.0. I also gained skills in programming and love working to optimize and automate tasks. I work primarily in the Python programming language and recently have started using the Django Framework but also have knowledge of JavaScript (React, jQuery), HTML, CSS (Bootstrap, Tailwind), SQL (postgreSQL), and OS command line languages (Powershell, Bash). I have a passion for learning and love the chance to gain new skills.

Looking ahead....

My prospects for the future are to start a career in web development focusing on backend using the Django Framework and to never lose the love of learning new skills. I am excited to see where my web development journey will take me and hope that those reading this will join me on that adventure.
